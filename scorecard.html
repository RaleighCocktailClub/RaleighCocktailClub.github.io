Balance
A perfectly balanced cocktail is divine. Is the drink too sour, too sweet or is it superbly balanced? 		/5

The glass and general visual appearance of a cocktail and garnish affect its appeal and points will be awarded accordingly. 		/5
Aroma
The aroma of the cocktail should be appealing and entice the drinker. 		/5
Taste
A cocktail should be pleasing. Is this one just palatable, taste refreshing or is it truly delicious? 		/10
Delivery
The interaction between bartender and customer should be efficient and stylish. Reward hygienic preparation as well as a confident and entertaining delivery with commentary that demonstrates knowledge. 		/5
