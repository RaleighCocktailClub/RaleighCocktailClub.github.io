Score Notes
Balance
A perfectly balanced cocktail is divine. Is the drink too sour, too sweet or is it superbly balanced? (0-5)
Presentation
The glass and general visual appearance of a cocktail and garnish affect its appeal and points will be awarded accordingly. (0-5)
Aroma
The aroma of the cocktail should be appealing and entice the drinker. (0-5)
Taste
A cocktail should be pleasing. Is this one just palatable, taste refreshing or is it truly delicious? (0-10)
Delivery
Interaction between bartender and customer is fundamental to good service and so judges should reward efficient, stylish and hygienic preparation as well as a confident and entertaining delivery with commentary that demonstrates knowledge. (0-5)
